## Chosen Approach: Dark Cinematic Luxury

### Design Movement
Post-digital luxury — a synthesis of cinematic film titles, sci-fi UI (Blade Runner 2049), and premium brand websites (Stripe, Linear, Vercel). The aesthetic communicates precision engineering and creative artistry simultaneously.

### Core Principles
1. **Depth through layers** — every surface has a backdrop, a blur, and a glow
2. **Motion as narrative** — animations tell a story, never just decorate
3. **Space is luxury** — generous whitespace creates breathing room and importance
4. **Particle poetry** — organic motion creates life and dimensionality

### Color Philosophy
- **Primary #7C3AED (Violet):** Authority and innovation — the signature color that pulses through the experience
- **Secondary #3B82F6 (Electric Blue):** Trust and technology — used for interactive states and accents
- **Accent #A855F7 (Purple):** Luxury and mystique — aurora gradient partner to violet
- **Background #050505:** Near-black void — creates infinite depth perception
- **Cards #111111:** Deep graphite — grounds glassmorphism elements
- **Text #FFFFFF:** Pure white — maximum contrast for readability
- **Subtext #A3A3A3:** Warm gray — hierarchy without competing

### Layout Paradigm
Asymmetric grids with generous negative space. Sections breathe with 120px+ vertical padding. Content rarely spans full width — 70-80% utilization with deliberate margins. Floating elements overlap sections for depth.

### Signature Elements
1. **Aurora Gradient Orbs** — Large, blurred, animated gradient spheres that float behind content sections
2. **Glassmorphism Cards** — Frosted glass surfaces with subtle borders and inner glow
3. **Particle Q Logo** — Animated particle system forming the brand mark

### Interaction Philosophy
Interactions should feel like touching a premium physical product — magnetic, responsive, with subtle haptic-like feedback. Hover reveals, scroll-triggered reveals, and cursor-following glows create an alive interface.

### Animation
- Page load: Particle Q formation (8-10s cinematic sequence)
- Scroll: Elements fade-up with 60ms stagger, parallax on background orbs
- Hover: Card tilt (3D perspective), glow follows cursor, scale(1.02)
- Buttons: Magnetic attraction, ripple on click, gradient shift
- Counters: Animated number counting with easing
- Transitions: Smooth page transitions with opacity crossfade

### Typography System
- **Headings:** Space Grotesk (700/800) — geometric, modern, authoritative
- **Body:** Inter (400/500) — clean, highly readable, professional
- **Numbers:** Satoshi-style numerals for statistics
- **Buttons:** Manrope (600) — friendly but confident
- **Hierarchy:** H1: 48-72px, H2: 36-48px, H3: 24-32px, Body: 16-18px

### Brand Essence
**"The digital architects of tomorrow's most memorable brands."** — For ambitious founders and enterprises who need their digital presence to match their vision. Different because we don't build websites — we engineer experiences.

**Personality:** Visionary, Precise, Magnetic

### Brand Voice
Headlines speak in bold, cinematic declarations. CTAs are confident invitations, not desperate pleas. Microcopy is sharp and purposeful.

**Example headline:** "We Build Digital Experiences That Feel Unforgettable."
**Example CTA:** "Explore Work" / "Book a Discovery Call"

### Wordmark & Logo
A particle-formed "Q" — thousands of glowing dots that orbit, converge, and breathe. The mark is dynamic, never static. On static applications, a minimal geometric Q with gradient stroke.

### Signature Brand Color
**#7C3AED** — A deep violet that bridges creativity and technology. It's the color that appears in hover glows, gradient transitions, and the pulsing core of the Q logo.

## Style Decisions
- Navbar: transparent → blurred glass on scroll
- Custom cursor: subtle glow dot that follows mouse
- Noise texture overlay on all dark backgrounds (CSS noise)
- Floating aurora blobs in hero and about sections
- Glassmorphism cards with backdrop-blur and subtle white border (white/10)
- Magnetic button effect on primary CTAs
- Every major page must include one cinematic depth moment (aurora orb, particle-Q geometry, volumetric light)
- Cards must be frosted graphite glass with violet/blue glow, fine luminous border, visible depth
- Brand copy must avoid generic agency phrasing, use cinematic engineering language
- More asymmetric cinematic composition: offset hero text, staggered card rows, overlapping layers
- Q/particle motif should recur across section dividers, timeline moments, portfolio hover, CTA backdrops
